History

Boyne RFC are a Drogheda based Rugby club. They play their homegames in Shamrock lodge. Boyne were founded in 1997 by a man called Mr Noel McQuillan after the amalgamation of Drogheda and Delvin RFC.

In the 2008-2009 season the Boyne first team claimed Ireland's oldest trophy the Leinster Towns Cup defeating Tullamore 27-21. Boyne retained the trophy in the 2009-2010 season defeating Tullamore again 32-27.

Boyne's very first competitive match was in the 1997/1998 season when they faced Skerries RFC at Bryanstown. Boyne's first Division 1 match came against Kilkenny RFC at Shmarock Lodge
